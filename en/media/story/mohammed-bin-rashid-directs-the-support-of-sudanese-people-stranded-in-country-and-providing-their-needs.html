Mohammed bin Rashid directs the support of Sudanese people stranded in country and providing their needs

His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, directed the support of Sudanese people stranded in country due to the ongoing conflict in Sudan, and providing their needs.

His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid directed the immediate support and follow-up of the Sudanese people stranded in the UAE, providing them with health care and shelter, in addition to working with all the competent authorities to ensure their safe return to their country at a later date.
